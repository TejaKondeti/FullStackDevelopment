How to Use the Farmer Dashboard

Step 1: Register

  1. Navigate to the Registration section.
  2. Enter a unique username and a secure password.
  3. Click on the Register button.
  4. You will receive a confirmation alert indicating successful registration.
Note: Make sure to remember your username and password for future logins.

Step 2: Login

  1. Go to the Login section.
  2. Enter your registered username and password.
  3. Click on the Login button.
  4. If your credentials are correct, you will be redirected to the Farmer Dashboard.
Note: If you forget your password, you will need to re-register as this demo does not support password recovery.

Step 3: Fill Your Dashboard

  1. In the Farmer Dashboard, you can add your products.
  2. Fill in the Product Name, Quantity, and Price.
  3. Click on the Submit Product button.
  4. Your product details will be displayed below the form.
Note: Ensure that the product details are accurate before submission.

Step 4: Marketers Make Proposals

  1. Once your product is listed, marketers can view it.
  2. Marketers can send buying proposals based on your listed products.
  3. You will receive an alert message with the proposal details.
Note: This feature is part of the backend logic and is not implemented in this demo.

Conclusion

Congratulations! You are now ready to use the Farmer Dashboard. Follow these steps to register, log in, and manage your products effectively.
